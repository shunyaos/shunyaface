Define your Product

Before you try turning your ideas into prototype, we ask you to write down your idea on a piece of paper. Just fill in these blanks.

I want to ________ with AI and take/trigger ___________ action.

or

I want to measure _____________ parameters with IoT and send _________ or take/trigger _________ action.

For Example :

I want to recognize faces with AI and trigger door lock to open when face recognized.

What can you do with our API's ?
